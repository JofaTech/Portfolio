About Me

Hi, I’m Brandon — a proud dad of three awesome girls and a lifelong tech enthusiast. I’ve always been curious about how things work, which started with coding simple text-based games as a kid and eventually led me to earn a Computer Science degree in 2022. Along the way, I’ve worn a lot of hats — from working in hardware engineering to managing systems support — but my heart’s set on building a career in software development.

  • Dual citizen, originally from Calgary, Alberta
  • Started programming young with text-based games
  • Background in Java, C++, front-end and back-end development
  • Degree in Electrical Engineering Technology + Computer Science
  • Experience with Agile, troubleshooting, and team collaboration
  • Former hardware engineer turned software-focused
  • Skilled in networking, software updates, and vendor relations
  • Current Systems Support Specialist managing tech for a school district
  • Passionate about clean code and practical problem-solving
  • Cooking is my creative outlet (love food TikToks & new recipes)
  • Enjoy reading sci-fi/fantasy, movies, and the occasional video game
  • Creativity shows up in writing, sketching, and playing guitar

I’m now focused on launching my career as a software developer or engineer — somewhere I can keep learning, contribute to meaningful work, and grow alongside a supportive team. I’m always up for a challenge, especially if it means building something useful and working with good people.
